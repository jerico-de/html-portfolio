Top 3 Series

1. Peaky Blinders

Peaky Blinders is a British crime drama television series created by Steven Knight. The series follows the exploits of the Shelby crime family in the aftermath of World War I. Known for its stylish cinematography, gripping storyline, and strong performances, Peaky Blinders has garnered a massive following worldwide.

2. Brooklyn Nine-Nine

Brooklyn Nine-Nine is an American police procedural comedy television series that revolves around the detectives of the fictional 99th precinct of the New York City Police Department. The show is praised for its humor, diverse cast, and positive representation of LGBTQ+ characters.
